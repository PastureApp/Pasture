Privacy Policy

Pasture — Rest in His Presence

Last updated: March 19, 2026

🕊️ Our Commitment to You

Pasture is a Christian Bible study and focus app designed to help you grow in faith. We are deeply committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains clearly and honestly how our app handles your information.

In short: we do not collect, store, or share your personal data. Your spiritual journey is private and belongs to you.

📱 Information We Do NOT Collect

Pasture does not collect any of the following:

• Your name, email address, or personal details

• Your location or device information

• Your prayer history, session logs, or focus records

• Any financial or payment information

• Any usage analytics or behavioral tracking data

💾 How Your Data is Stored

All app data — including your session history, earned tokens, fruit progress, streak counts, and wisdom path progress — is stored locally on your own device using your browser's built-in storage (localStorage).

This data never leaves your device and is never transmitted to any server, third party, or external service.

If you clear your browser data or uninstall the app, your local progress will be deleted.

🤝 Third-Party Services

Pasture uses Google Fonts to display beautiful typography. Google Fonts may collect anonymized usage data according to Google's own privacy policy. No personally identifiable information is shared.

We do not use any advertising networks, analytics platforms, or data brokers.

👨‍👩‍👧 Children's Privacy

Pasture is a faith-based app suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. Since we collect no personal data from any users, children are equally protected.

🔒 Security

Since Pasture does not collect or transmit personal data, there is no data at risk of breach on our end. Your progress is stored on your own device, under your own control.

📝 Changes to This Policy

If we ever update this privacy policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this page periodically. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
